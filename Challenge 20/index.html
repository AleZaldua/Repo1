TRAVEL ASSISTME APP

A Travel Planning & Budgeting App

Travelers post- pandemic are struggling to plan their upcoming trips because of the amount of new things they have to keep in mind, to a point that they feel overwhelmed, preferring to postpone the journey. This APP was developed to be a trip planner app that brought users a straightforward solution to organize next trips while keep an eye on their budget.

My Role: Product Designer-User research, Design, Prototyping and User testing.

Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop

User Research

Overview

After the pandemic we have seen a rise in traveling around the world. People are finally able to fly and travel, wasting no time to get out of their homes to do whatever is on their bucket lists. Our main goal was to explore and understand traveler's habits and attitudes towards traveling post pandemic. We conducted 6 remotely moderated interviews The ideal participant were those who can consider themselves people who enjoy traveling and got the chase to do it during and after pandemic.

Results & Top Insights

We discovered that planning (if is possible several months ahead) and budgeting (clear idea of how much they will spend) were two important factors in the top of mind of users.

Creating a User Persona

For a better understanding of all the information and user's insights, we use an Affinity Diagram to cluster and categorize the results.

USER PERSONA: Vanessa B. Overwhelmed

Who is Vanessa? What they need?

Vanessa is eager to plan her trips but doesn't know where to start.

  1. They have a lot of trouble organizing all their plans because of the unlimited things to be done.
  2. Thinking of all the costs for budgeting is overwhelming.
  3. Wants straightforward, easy and clear information for planning the trip.
  4. Wanting to see comparisons with flight, accommodation, attraction prices.

Ideation Process

To enhance comprehension, we clustered and selected the most relevant ideas that would help the users. Finally using a Feature Prioritization Matrix to classify and validate those ideas.
