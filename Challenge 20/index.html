TRAVEL ASSISTME APP

A Travel Planning & Budgeting App

Travelers post- pandemic are struggling to plan their upcoming trips because of the amount of new things they have to keep in mind, to a point that they feel overwhelmed, preferring to postpone the journey. This APP was developed to be a trip planner app that brought users a straightforward solution to organize next trips while keep an eye on their budget.

My Role: Product Designer-User research, Design, Prototyping and User testing.

Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop

User Research

Overview

After the pandemic we have seen a rise in traveling around the world. People are finally able to fly and travel, wasting no time to get out of their homes to do whatever is on their bucket lists. Our main goal was to explore and understand traveler's habits and attitudes towards traveling post pandemic. We conducted 6 remotely moderated interviews The ideal participant were those who can consider themselves people who enjoy traveling and got the chase to do it during and after pandemic.

Results & Top Insights

We discovered that planning (if is possible several months ahead) and budgeting (clear idea of how much they will spend) were two important factors in the top of mind of users.

Testing & Iterating

We tested 3 different tasks to the initial prototype on several participants.

Overall Results: 70% of success rate on the first task, 60% of success rate on the second task and 40% of success rate on the third task.

User Testing Results: Feedback

  • The Onboarding and sign up process were pretty straightforward. Similar to other procedures of other common apps.
  • Good work on the UX Writing content. Users felt like it has a human touch.
  • Overall, the flow it's good but it needs a homepage to connect the dots in users. Without the homepage, users felt “lost” like they don't know “where” they are.
  • Creation of a Add to Cart button and Filter mode
  • Make more visible the Budget Tracker feature.
  • Give more room to each booking areas. Potentially, create 1 page per area.
    • Flights
    • Accommodations
    • Tours
    • Rental Cars
  • Make the bigger pictures and less text with the option of “view more”.

FINAL PROTOTYPE

Lessons Learned

Feedback is your BEST FRIEND:

Its funny how you think that you have the best user flow until you test it. We learned valuable lessons about not complicate ourselves with procedures but make it simple and concise. We thought by not implementing a homepage and by go directly to booking it will be the best choice. However users did not understand the process. Accept mistakes and have an open mind is crucial to determine a better path to go.

Iteration is the WAY:

It's easy to being in love of your idea and believe that is working. It is important to consider second choices and be open to implement new decision supported always by the research. Next time we will designate more time to do more testing / iteration to achieve the maximal goal.
