TRAVEL ASSISTME APP

A Travel Planning & Budgeting App

Travelers post- pandemic are struggling to plan their upcoming trips because of the amount of new things they have to keep in mind, to a point that they feel overwhelmed, preferring to postpone the journey. This APP was developed to be a trip planner app that brought users a straightforward solution to organize next trips while keep an eye on their budget.

My Role: Product Designer-User research, design, prototyping and user testing.

Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop
